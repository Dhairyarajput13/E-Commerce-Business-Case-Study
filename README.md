# E-Commerce-Business-Case-Study
Designed and executed SQL queries on Customers, Products, Orders, and Order Details datasets to simulate real-world e-commerce analysis.
Delivered customer insights by identifying Delhi, Chennai, and Jaipur as key cities for marketing focus.
Performed product performance analysis to optimize stock and sales trend analysis showing February 2024 decline and December growth.
Improved inventory management by tracking Product 7 turnover and minimizing excess inventory through planned restocking.
Tech Stack: SQL, Relational Databases, Business Analytics
